About Me

I am an astronomer who is interested in studying the evolution of stars. My research focuses on binary star systems, with a particular interest in stars stripped in binaries and their evolution. I have experience in modeling stellar evolution and atmospheres. I am currently pursuing my PhD, where I aim to deepen our understanding of stellar structure, mass loss, and the complex processes in binary systems.

Research Interests

  • Stellar structure with MESA
  • Stellar atmospheres & radiative transfer
  • Spectroscopic modeling with FASTWIND and CMFGEN
  • Binary interactions & stripped star evolution
