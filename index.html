About Me

I am an astronomer studying the evolution of stars, focusing on binary systems and stripped stars. My work combines stellar evolution and atmosphere modeling to understand how mass loss and binary interactions shape stellar lifetimes. I use tools like MESA and FASTWIND to explore how stars evolve, transfer mass, and reveal their inner physics through spectra.
